Welcome to the website for the Climbing Team at the University of Virginia.

We are a competitive and recreational rock climbing team composed of both undergraduate and graduate students at UVA. Our mission is to provide members with access to rad outdoor and indoor rock climbing and competitive training. We practice twice a week and try to get outside as much as possible! As a team, we compete in the USA Climbing Collegiate Series, Triple Crown Bouldering Series, as well as other local competitions and travel to world class rock such as the New River Gorge, Red River Gorge, Grayson Highlands State Park, Stone Fort, HP 40, and more!
